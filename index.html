Turon

15 pesos


Turon is a ubiquitous street food
enjoyed by Filipinos of all ages.
It is a popular afternoon snack,
sold by street vendors or peddled
within barangays by entrepreneurial
mothers. Turon is made of sliced
saba bananas, wrapped and
rolled in spring roll wrappers
then deep-fried until golden.
